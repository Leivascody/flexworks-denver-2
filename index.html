Investor FAQ

Questions we get asked

What if rents don't move to market as quickly as projected?

The sensitivity analysis shows a -10% market rent scenario still delivers 8.1% net investor IRR / 1.4x EM. Our underwriting also assumes elevated physical vacancy and rental concessions in years 1-2 to account for tenant turnover — far more conservative than what we've seen at FlexWorks Denver I where 99% occupancy held during repositioning.

Why is this priced so close to Beach Street despite Beach's strong performance?

The seller is a hands-off private owner who under-manages for occupancy rather than rent. Beach's $126 PSF basis was acquired in a similar dynamic two years ago. The 4% premium reflects time value and Beach's improved comp data — not a sponsor markup.

What's the downside on the building's age (1985/86)?

Closing conditions in the PSA obligate the seller to complete or credit major deferred maintenance (asphalt/paving, unit heaters, insulation, roof). We're carrying the full $673k estimate as a conservative reserve and expect to reduce the budget post-DD.

What's the GSC co-investment in this deal?

Manager co-invest is 10–15% of equity ($250k minimum, 10% required). GSC's economics are aligned with investors through co-investment, an 8% preferred return, and a 60/40 GP/LP split only above a 15% IRR.

How liquid is this investment?

Flex-storage leases are typically 6-12 months, giving us flexibility on reversion timing. Base case is a 5-year hold; there are no illiquid windows due to major tenants or remaining lease terms. A successful refi at year 2 returns capital while preserving the equity position.

What's the depreciation / tax treatment?

Upon acquisition, GSC will perform a cost-segregation analysis to accelerate depreciation. This typically generates significant paper losses in years 1-3 that offset cash distributions, materially improving the after-tax IRR for high-tax-bracket investors. Consult your tax advisor for personalized treatment.

How do you handle environmental risk on an older industrial building?

Pre-PSA, the seller performed Phase I and Phase II ESA assessments. Phase II scope was prepared by GSC and our environmental consultant — came back clean with no remediation required. We're phasing out the small number of auto-related tenants from the rent roll over the lease cycle.

What happens if interest rates move significantly?

Acquisition debt is SOFR + 80 bps with a 3-year interest rate cap acquired at close. Stabilization refinance is sized to a conservative 12% debt yield at T+225 — we expect actual refi terms to price tighter (9-10% debt yield), creating refi upside not in the base case.
